Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 100g Diced Pancetta
  • 100g Spaghetti
  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • 40g Parmesan
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add oil to a hot pan and begin frying the pancetta
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling, salted water (should take around 10 minutes)
  3. While your pancetta and spaghetti are cooking, add your egg yolks to a bowl and beat thouroughly
  4. Add your parmesan to beaten egg yolks and mix well
  5. When your pancetta is cripsy, remove pan from heat
  6. When spaghetti is al dente, add 2 tbsp of the water to the pancetta, and drain spaghetti
  7. Add salt and pepper to your egg mix, and add drained spaghetti and egg mix to your pancetta pan
  8. Mix everything together thouroughly. The residual heat from the pan should cook the eggs without scrambling them
  9. Plate your spaghetti, top with grated parmesan and black pepper, and enjoy!
